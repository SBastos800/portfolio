About me

Hello! I'm Sonia. I'm naturally inquisitive, and in my professional life I'm dedicated and have always pursued my dreams. I was born in Portugal, and I grew up near Porto, which is where I went to the University. After I graduated as a nurse, I spent four and a half years working in a National Hospital in Lisbon. I loved living in Lisbon, but eventually, drawn by the English language and career opportunities, I decided to move to London to take a role as a Staff Nurse in one of the most renowned Private Hospitals in London. I fully settled in London, and after a further 8 years working in the healthcare sector, I've leapt into the world of Tech and am now an aspiring Trainee Web Developer. I'm passionate about new experiences and when experiencing feeling of the unknown, my curiosity gives me courage to keep going!
